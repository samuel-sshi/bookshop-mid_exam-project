About Us

We are a team consist of three people who have the same interest in the Korean language. We have studied most of our lives in Korea and learn their culture. We believe that we can connect with a culture through it's language. So, we decide to help people that have the same interest in the Korean language by publishing these books.


We have made a lot of improvements to the new books, we try to make it more attractive to millenials who give an interest to the Hallyu Wave. Fun and interesting is the main theme of these books. We spend most of our lives working together and that makes us a great team. We hope that everyone enjoys the Korean language by learning from our books as we enjoyed making it.
See you all on the other side!
